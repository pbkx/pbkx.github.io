projects photography other

i often choose distraction as the best form of ignorance
the world is often terrifying

i’m tired - lying at the edge of my bed, heart unable to decide
its fate; between the depths of the covers or up past the rain-clouds
that obscure a vision of the flames of heaven

calm is abstract - a concept that i have failed to grasp
always busy; a city rat with little purpose but gnawing through
an ever-growing stack of sheets; the end always just out of reach

are those who appreciate me here to stay?
so far, they’ve all disappeared
for a valiant few, i’m unsure of secret resentment or true approval

yet they are never what i’m looking for
the present is never satisfying

“now” is an unsatisfying image of the past;
what was once despised, now faded
leaving you wondering, how you lacked joy

the future being a foggy prospect
the hope for greatness, demeaning the now
occludes today’s reality with a dream of eventual satisfaction

knowing it’s not true.
inevitable discontent.
inescapable distress.

music offers a calming escape from the tumultuous present,
yet i never find the time to make my own
i tell myself it will happen soon.

yet, i always do.
